The DETAIL team

A close-knit group of researchers spanning hardware, machine learning, and systems. 👋

Director · Principal Investigator

Dr. Xun Jiao

Assistant Professor · Dept. of Electrical & Computer Engineering, Villanova University

Leads the Dependable, Efficient, and Intelligent Computing Lab (DETAIL), using a software-hardware codesign approach to build dependable and efficient computing systems for the applications of machine intelligence. Research spans robust/resilient AI/ML systems, hardware reliability and approximate computing, fuzz testing, and brain-inspired hyperdimensional computing. Our research is sponsored by NSF, NIH, L3Harris, and NVidia.
